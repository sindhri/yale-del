The Yale Child Study Center’s affiliation with the Anna Freud Centre is a result of it’s shared mission to keep abreast of cutting-edge research in psychoanalysis and related fields so as to better inform treatment of children and adolescents with emotional and behavioral problems. It is hoped that the international alliances formed between these and other research institutes will serve as a model of interdisciplinary and international collaboration in child mental health. In addition to her position as the Arnold Gesell Professor of child psychiatry, psychology, and pediatrics, Dr. Mayes is Chair of the Anna Freud Centre’s Directorial Team.
